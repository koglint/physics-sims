Formula Derivation

Ideal Banked Curve Speed

Reveal the derivation of v = √(r g tan θ) one step at a time.

Back to simulation
Step 1

Resolve the normal force into components on the banked road.

Step 2

FN cos θ = m g

Step 3

FN sin θ = m v2 / r

Step 4

Divide the horizontal equation by the vertical equation.

Step 5

(FN sin θ) / (FN cos θ) = (m v2 / r) / (m g)

Step 6

tan θ = v2 / (r g)

Step 7

v2 = r g tan θ

Step 8

v = √(r g tan θ)
